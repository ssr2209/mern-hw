Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and captains the Portugal national team. He is widely considered one of the greatest players of all time.

Early life

Cristiano Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. He grew up in a modest household and was the youngest of four children. From a young age, he showed immense passion and talent for football. At age 12, he moved to Lisbon to join Sporting CP's youth academy, marking the start of his professional journey. He was diagnosed with a racing heart at 15, which required surgery, but he recovered fully and continued playing.

Club career

Sporting CP (2002-2003): Debuted professionally in Portugal. His skill caught global attention. Manchester United (2003-2009): Signed by Sir Alex Ferguson. Won 3 Premier League titles, a Champions League (2008), and his first Ballon d'Or (2008). Real Madrid (2009-2018): Transferred for a then-world record €94M. Scored 450 goals in 438 matches, winning 4 Champions League titles and 4 Ballon d'Ors. Juventus (2018-2021): Moved for €100M. Won 2 Serie A titles, scored over 100 goals. Manchester United (2021-2022): Returned briefly. Mixed spell under different managers. Al Nassr (2023-Present): Joined the Saudi club, becoming the face of the Saudi Pro League expansion. Career Club Stats (as of mid-2025): Total Club Goals: 740+ Total Appearances: 1,100+

International career

Ronaldo made his debut for Portugal in 2003. He became the nation's all-time top scorer with 128+ goals. Major Achievements: Euro 2016 Champion Nations League 2019,2025 Winner He has played in 5 FIFA World Cups (2006-2022) and 5 UEFA Euros (2004–2024), making him one of the most capped players in international history.

Style of play

Ronaldo is known for his: Explosive pace, leaping ability, and powerful shooting. Exceptional heading ability and clinical finishing. Transitioned from a tricky winger in his early career to a prolific goal-scoring forward. Renowned for dedication, fitness, and work ethic.

Personal life

Partner: Georgina Rodríguez Children: Ronaldo has 5 children, some born via surrogacy. Known for his strict diet and fitness routine, he's often praised for his discipline. A global icon, he has numerous endorsements (e.g., Nike, CR7 brand). Actively involved in charity, including donations to hospitals and children's causes. He’s one of the most followed people on social media worldwide.
