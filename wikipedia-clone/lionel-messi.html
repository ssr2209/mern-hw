Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and captains the Argentina national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time.

Early life

Lionel Messi was born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Santa Fe, Argentina. At age 11, he was diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency. His family couldn’t afford the treatment, but FC Barcelona saw his potential and offered to pay for his medical expenses if he joined their youth academy. Messi moved to Spain at age 13 and trained at La Masia, Barcelona’s famed football academy.

Club career

FC Barcelona (2004-2021): Messi made his senior debut at age 17. He spent 17 years at the club, becoming its all-time top scorer with 672 goals in 778 appearances. Major Titles: 10 La Liga, 4 Champions League, 7 Copa del Rey Won 7 Ballon d'Ors during his Barcelona years. Paris Saint-Germain (2021-2023): Moved after Barça's financial issues. Scored 32 goals in 75 games and won 2 Ligue 1 titles. Inter Miami (2023-Present): Joined the MLS club in 2023. Helped boost U.S. football popularity and won the Leagues Cup 2023. Career Club Stats (as of mid-2025): Total Club Goals: 740+ Total Appearances: 1,050+

International career

Messi debuted for Argentina in 2005. Initially criticized for not winning major trophies, he later silenced critics by leading Argentina to major wins: Copa América 2021,2025 Champion FIFA World Cup 2022 Winner Argentina's all-time top scorer with 110+ goals. Played in 5 World Cups (2006-2022) and several Copa América tournaments.

Style of play

Messi is known for: Dribbling ability, close control, and vision. Playing as a false nine, attacking midfielder, or right winger. Exceptional passing, playmaking, and goal-scoring instincts. Renowned for his left foot, quick changes of direction, and football IQ. Contrasts Ronaldo's power-based game with grace and creativity.

Personal life

Wife: Antonela Roccuzzo Children: 3 sons - Thiago, Mateo, and Ciro Known for a private lifestyle, deep family values, and charitable work. Founded the Leo Messi Foundation to support access to education and health for children. Messi has endorsement deals with brands like Adidas, Pepsi, and Gatorade. Considered one of the greatest footballers in history, admired worldwide for both skill and humility.
